Quick Answers

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to be from Alfaisal to apply? ＋

ONCORE is primarily for Alfaisal University medical students. If you're at another Saudi university and interested, contact us — we may consider exceptional cases.

How long until I hear back? ＋

For students: we screen profiles within 7-10 days, then forward shortlists to faculty as projects open. For faculty: we publish your listing within 48 hours of submission.

Is this paid research? ＋

Most projects offer co-authorship and research experience rather than financial compensation. Some funded projects do offer stipends — these are clearly marked in the listing.

What if my GPA is low? ＋

Apply anyway. We weigh motivation, prior research interest, and the quality of your "why oncology" answer heavily. GPA is one of many signals, not a gatekeeper. ONCORE actively prioritizes students with limited prior research exposure.

Can I be on multiple projects at once? ＋

Yes, but we recommend starting with one project and seeing it through. Faculty value reliability over volume.

Are there workshops or training? ＋

Yes — ONCORE delivers structured research workshops led by experienced students and professionals, focused on research methodology, critical appraisal, and scientific writing.
