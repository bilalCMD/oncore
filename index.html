The Process

Four steps from question to collaboration.

A structured process modelled after academic recruitment — faculty post their research, students apply, our coordinators shortlist, and research begins.

Step 01

Faculty Posts

Oncologists submit their research project, required skills, expected duration, and the kind of student they're looking for.

Step 02

Students Apply

Members submit their CV, year of study, prior research experience, and the specific projects they're interested in.

Step 03

ONCORE Shortlists

Our research coordinators review applicants and shortlist the best matches based on the faculty's stated requirements.

Step 04

Research Begins

The faculty member reviews the shortlist, picks their team, and a structured collaboration begins with clear deliverables.

Our Four Core Disciplines

Working across oncology's frontline.

ONCORE operates across four core oncology disciplines at KFSHRC. Through close collaboration with consultants and research teams, student placements are tailored to each department's specific needs.

01

Medical Oncology

Chemotherapy protocols, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, and clinical trial outcomes.

02

Radiation Oncology

Treatment planning, outcomes research, advanced techniques like SBRT and IMRT.

03

Hematology-Oncology

Leukemia, lymphoma, myeloma, transplant outcomes, and translational research.

04

Palliative Care

Quality of life research, symptom management, advance care planning, and patient outcomes.

A Strategic Partnership

Backed by world-class institutions in Riyadh.

KFSHRC
Centre of Excellence in Oncology
Alfaisal University
College of Medicine, Riyadh

ONCORE operates under a unique partnership between Alfaisal University's College of Medicine and the King Faisal Specialist Hospital & Research Centre — ranked 2nd in the Middle East by Newsweek and treating approximately 25% of Saudi Arabia's cancer cases annually.

This collaboration ensures every research placement happens at a Centre of Excellence — with mentorship from leading oncologists and exposure to cutting-edge clinical work.
